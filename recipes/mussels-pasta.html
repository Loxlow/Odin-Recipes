Mussels and Clams in a White Wine Sauce

This recipe is one of my wife's favorites. We use frozen mussels from the Publix seafood section, and other easy ingredients to make this dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 package frozen mussels
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup white cooking wine
  • 1 can diced clams in juice
  • 1 splash lemon juice
  • 1 pound linguine
  • fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure

  1. Thaw mussels in the kitchen sink whilst you boil noodles and dice the garlic and shallots.
  2. Boil 2 quarts of water in a 4 quart pot, add up to 4 tablespoons salt. Boil the noodles to al dente whilst making the sauce.
  3. Sautee the shallots and garlic in olive oil for several minutes, until fragrant and soft.
  4. Add the white wine and diced clams, including the clam can juice
  5. Add mussels to sauce, cover and cook over medium boil for 5 or so minutes
  6. Add splash of lemon juice anytime
  7. Add noodles to sauce, letting them soak some sauce in for 2 minutes whilst occasionally mixing
